How Blockchain Improves This Business

Blockchain improves the art business by creating trust, transparency, and secure ownership records. In the traditional art market, fake paintings and duplicate copies can be sold as originals. This creates financial losses for buyers and damages the reputation of artists. ArtChain solves this problem by assigning every artwork a unique digital certificate stored on the blockchain. Once registered, the certificate cannot be changed or deleted easily.

Whenever the artwork is sold, ownership is transferred through the blockchain, creating a permanent history of transactions. Buyers can verify whether the art piece is genuine and who owned it previously. This increases confidence in online and offline purchases.

Artists also benefit because smart contracts can automatically give royalty payments each time the artwork is resold. Galleries and auction houses save time in verifying documents. Collectors get secure proof of ownership. Overall, blockchain modernizes the art industry by reducing fraud, improving trust, and ensuring fair rewards for creators.
